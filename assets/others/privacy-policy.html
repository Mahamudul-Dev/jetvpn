JetVPN — Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 19, 2025

1) Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how JetVPN handles user data when you use our VPN service. By using JetVPN, you agree to this Policy.

2) No Account Registration

You do not need to create an account. Device IP address is used to automatically register your session with our servers.

3) Data We Collect

  • Device IP address: used for server registration and connection management.
  • Payment details: handled securely by Google Play and Apple App Store; JetVPN does not store your payment information.
  • Minimal technical logs: may include session duration and server load balancing information. We do not log browsing activity or VPN traffic content.

4) How We Use Your Data

  • Provide VPN connectivity and server access.
  • Maintain service stability and security.
  • Process subscription payments (via App Stores).

5) Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only when required by law or to protect the security of our services.

6) Payments

All payments are processed by Google Play and Apple App Store. Please review their privacy policies for more details.

7) Security

We apply industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no internet-based service can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8) Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13, or the age of digital consent in your region. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

10) Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@jetvpn.example
